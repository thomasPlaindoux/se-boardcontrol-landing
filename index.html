What's inside

Everything an SE needs.
Nothing they don't.

Every feature was designed around a real SE workflow — morning pipeline review, in-meeting notes, DSR compliance, team visibility. No bloat, no fluff.

Control Room
Your full pipeline in one view. Stage progress bars, SE fields, scorecard, DSR status — all inline-editable and synced to Org62 instantly.
Slack-Markdown Notes
Type ## for a header, - for a list. Notes auto-save, export to Slack mrkdwn in one click, and never interrupt your flow.
Gemini AI — Built In
1-1 meeting prep briefings, SE field generation, note summarization, scorecard suggestions — all from your own Gemini API key, never shared.
DSR Tracker
Color-coded overdue scoring, action items surfaced by age, priority matrix. Never miss a DSR again — warnings appear the moment you open the app.
My Agenda + Org62 Sync
Google Calendar events directly in the app. Stage them for sync, commit to Org62 in one click. Hours tracking, won't-sync rules, full audit log.
Global Search (⌘K)
One keystroke to search across all deals, accounts, AEs, contacts. Deep search across your entire pipeline — results appear in under 100ms.
Account Planning
Per-account notes, documents, transcripts — all organized in folders. Activity history pulled live from Salesforce. Everything in one place, always.
Team Growth View
Your team's pipeline at a glance. Filter by AE, role, deal band. See who's hitting hygiene and who needs attention — before your manager does.
Health Score
Real-time health ring combining hours logged, DSR compliance, and opp freshness. Tells you exactly where to focus in 3 seconds flat.
How it works

Open it in the morning.
It's ready before you are.

No login. No loading spinners on every click. No tab-switching between Salesforce, Calendar, and Slack. Everything loads once, everything saves instantly.

Morning — Pipeline snapshot in 10s
Open the app. Control Room loads your full pipeline from Org62 in the background while you read the health score. DSR warnings are already flagged.
Before a meeting — AI briefing in 30s
Hit "AI Brief" on any 1-1. Gemini reads the opp data, your notes, and upcoming events — and generates a structured briefing ready to paste into Slack.
In the meeting — notes that don't interrupt
Switch to Deal Notes. Type naturally — Slack markdown shortcuts work instantly. Auto-saved every keystroke. The UI disappears, your content stays.
End of day — Org62 sync in one click
Your calendar events are staged and waiting. Review them in the Agenda tab, hit Commit — all activity is logged to Salesforce. Done.

Security & reliability

Local-first. Private by design.

SE Board Control runs entirely on your laptop. Your Salesforce token, your notes, your pipeline data — none of it leaves your machine unless you explicitly sync to Org62.

Runs locally
Python server on your machine. No cloud backend, no SaaS vendor, no data at rest anywhere but localhost.
Official OAuth only
Salesforce and Google connections use standard OAuth 2.0. Tokens stored locally, auto-refresh, revocable any time from the Settings tab.
Your Gemini key
AI features use your own Gemini API key stored in ~/.gemini_api_key. No shared inference endpoint, no prompt leakage.
Automatic daily backup to Slack
Your deal notes, safe in Slack — even if your laptop burns

SE Board Control stores all notes locally — which is great for privacy, but means they only live on your machine. The SE Note Backup Slack Workflow runs every day and automatically pushes your deal notes as Slack Canvases, one per opportunity. They're searchable, shareable, and survive a laptop wipe. Each canvas is tagged #seboardnote for easy retrieval.

How it works
1 Copy the Slack Workflow to your workspace (button below)
2 Configure the webhook URL in SE Board Control Settings
3 Notes sync to Slack Canvases automatically every day

Get started

Up and running
in 60 seconds.

There's a one-click installer for macOS. Or do it manually if you prefer full control.

1
Request access on Slack

2
Install — one click or one command
The automatic installer handles Python, dependencies, Launch Agent (auto-start), and PATH for you.
Recommended macOS · auto-installs everything
# Paste in Terminal — installs & launches the app bash <(curl -sL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/thomasPlaindoux/sf-se-boardcontrol/main/dashboard/install.sh)
Installs Python deps, creates a se-dashboard command, registers a macOS Launch Agent so the server auto-starts at login, and opens the app.
Manual install (for techies)
# Clone after getting access from Thomas git clone <repo-url> se-boardcontrol cd se-boardcontrol/dashboard pip install -r requirements.txt
# Start python3 server_optimized.py open http://localhost:8080
3
Connect Salesforce & Google Calendar
Open the app, go to Settings — one-click OAuth for both. The Welcome screen walks you through it.
4
Add your Gemini API key (optional)
For AI features — meeting prep, note summaries, SE field suggestions. Free tier at aistudio.google.com is more than enough.
